# ASTRA — Project Identity & Metadata

## Project Name
**ASTRA** (Adaptive Smart Traffic Response & Analytics)

---

## Tagline
> **Predict. Plan. Divert. Respond.**

---

## One-Line Description
ASTRA is an AI-powered Traffic Operations Intelligence Platform that helps city authorities forecast event-driven congestion, optimize resource deployment, and generate diversion strategies through predictive analytics and geospatial intelligence.

---

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
ASTRA (Adaptive Smart Traffic Response & Analytics) transforms traffic management from reactive response to predictive operations. By combining machine learning, spatial intelligence, historical event retrieval, and decision-support systems, ASTRA forecasts traffic impact, recommends police deployment and barricading strategies, and generates diversion plans before congestion escalates.

---

## Resume / Project Summary Version
Built ASTRA (Adaptive Smart Traffic Response & Analytics), an AI-powered traffic operations intelligence platform that predicts event severity, estimates resolution times, retrieves similar historical incidents, recommends traffic resource allocation, and generates diversion strategies using machine learning and geospatial analytics.
